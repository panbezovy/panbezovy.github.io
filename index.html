About

Who?

Hello! My name is Martin Psota and I am from Ivancice. I have studied... well I still study, but I try to start work on my own. My first collage was Mendel University in Brno, where I studied Furniture Design. Now I study Spatial Design on Tomas Bata University in Zlin, because I want to be better and create magnificent things! I like music, I have a really big LP collection, which every day grows larger. One of my dreams is swimming in a pool full of puding, well this one is the funniest, the most important dream is helping people with their interior design and making them happy :).

What?

Well, what actually am I capable of? I am skilful guy, who does every sort of work! I can illustrate, draw and also do a vector graphic. I mostly use Adobe. I also work with 3D programs like Sketchup or Rhinoceros, so if you wish some kitchen, lamp or other product design, contact me please, I am grateful for every sort of work.
